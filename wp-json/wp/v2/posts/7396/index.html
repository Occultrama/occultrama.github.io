\nSecond Sight Paranormal TV:

1. Tub of Sorrow featuring the home of Earl Winfield Spencer Jr. and Wallis Warfield Simpson. Simpson famously went on to marry England’s, King Edward VIII.

\n2. Caskets to Cafe featuring Second Sight Paranormal and Forensic Services and International Psychic Medium Ericka Boussarhane as they explore the ghostly occurrences of a closed cafe that was formally the home of Louis and Clara Levy.

\n3. Murder and Mayhem Murder and Mayhem featuring Second Sight Paranormal and Forensic Services and International Psychic Medium Ericka Boussarhane as they explore the haunted history, murder, and debauchery in downtown Pensacola.

\n4. Ghost Keepers of the Light featuring Second Sight Paranormal and Forensic Services and International Psychic Medium Ericka Boussarhane as they visit the National Naval Aviation Museum and Pensacola Lighthouse.

\n5. Echoes of War featuring Second Sight Paranormal and Forensic Services and International Psychic Medium Ericka Boussarhane as they discover the apparitions of soldiers and ghostly echos of war at Fort Morgan and Fort Blakeley.

\n6. Eternal Playtime features Second Sight Paranormal and Forensic Services and International Psychic Medium Ericka Boussarhane as they travel to Mobile, Alabama to investigate the ghostly occurrences at the DAR House, built-in 1860 by Riverboat Captain Charles G. and Elizabeth Steele Richards.

\n7. Bon Secour: Spirits of the River features Second Sight Paranormal and Forensic Services and International Psychic Medium Ericka Boussarhane as they investigate the ghostly history of the Swift-Coles House in Bon Secour, Alabama. *In memory of previous owners, Charles and Susan Swift, and Nicholas Coles.

\n8. The Haunting of the Malaga Inn features Second Sight Paranormal and Forensic Services and International Psychic Medium Ericka Boussarhane as they find secrets and spirits at the Malaga Inn in Mobile, Alabama.<\/p>\n
