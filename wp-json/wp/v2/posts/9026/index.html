The Astrology of Pandemics is a 42-minute documentary film on the astrology of both COVID-19 and previous pandemics. An unprecedented astronomical and astrological journey that investigates the same planetary configuration that happens at each pandemic to date. Can astrology help us understand the current pandemic and assist us in preparing for future outbreaks? Featuring an interview with renowned astrologer Andr\u00e9 Barbault who, back in 2011, predicted the 2020 pandemic, using planetary cycles.<\/p>\n
