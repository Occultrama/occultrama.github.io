Disfigured, disfigured, Tom turned his degenerative disease into play, creating bark masks inspired by Commedia Dell’arte. Then Tom is gone. He left the field to go out, far from all. Adult, Alain has left behind this part of the past. Until the phone call. At the end of the line, a staff hits twelve times. Tom came back to finish his performance. Tonight, the masks will fall.<\/p>\n
