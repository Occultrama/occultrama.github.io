Sasha is obsessed with death, necrophilia, and decaying human bodies. In an otherworldly forest, he disturbs a shaman who makes the line between our world and the demon realm shift. At night the situation spirals out of control, throwing the world into an eternal night of horrors. To save himself, Sasha ventures into a world inhabited by tormenting vampires and seductive demons. In the original version from 2006 of Andrey Iskanov’s Visions of Suffering, demons stalk two men in their dreams and threaten to break through into their waking world. Note: This version is significantly different than Visions of Suffering: Final Director’s Cut, which featured new characters and a different narrative.<\/p>\n
